MONKEY TRAIL

Step into the playful world of primates on Monkey Trail, an exciting, winding path that takes you through a vibrant habitat filled with curious, energetic monkeys. Designed to feel like a walk through the jungle, this interactive experience lets you observe different monkey species up close as they swing, climb, and chatter through the treetops.

Monkey Trail is home to a variety of monkey species including capuchins, squirrel monkeys, and howler monkeys. Elevated walkways and glass-viewing domes give you unique perspectives and incredible photo opportunities.

Educational signs along the trail offer fun facts about monkey behavior, social structures, and conservation efforts. Kids can also enjoy interactive learning stations and jungle-themed play zones scattered throughout the trail.
