THE LOST FOREST

Take a break from your zoo adventure and discover The Lost Forest, a peaceful picnic area nestled beneath tall trees and lush greenery. This hidden gem offers a quiet retreat where families and friends can relax, recharge, and enjoy a meal surrounded by nature.

With plenty of shaded seating, rustic wooden tables, and soft grassy areas perfect for picnic blankets, The Lost Forest provides a scenic escape from the busier parts of the zoo. You might even spot a few curious birds or butterflies fluttering by while you eat!

The area is fully equipped with nearby restrooms, recycling bins, and handwashing stations to make your visit comfortable and eco-friendly. Feel free to bring your own lunch or grab takeout from the Zoo Diner or Coffee Shopâ€”both are just a short walk away.

Whether you're planning a group outing, celebrating a birthday, or simply enjoying a quiet snack, The Lost Forest is the perfect place to unwind and reconnect with nature.

Open during all zoo hours. Please remember to clean up after yourself and keep the forest wild and welcoming for all visitors.
