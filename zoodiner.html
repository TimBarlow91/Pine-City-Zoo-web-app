THE ZOO DINER

Take a break from your wildlife adventure and grab a bite at the The Zoo Diner! Located at the heart of the zoo, the diner offers a variety of delicious meals, snacks, and refreshing beverages for the whole family. Whether you're in the mood for a juicy burger, a fresh salad, or a kid-friendly combo meal, there's something for everyone on the menu.

The Zoo Diner is a perfect place to relax and recharge in a comfortable, animal-themed atmosphere. With indoor and outdoor seating available, you can enjoy your meal while watching flamingos nearby or listening to the sounds of nature. Don't forget to try our famous "Safari Sundae" or pick up a themed snack box for the kids!
