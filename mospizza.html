MO's PIZZA

If you're craving something cheesy and delicious during your zoo visit, stop by Mo's Pizza—a family favorite serving up fresh, hot pizzas all day long! Conveniently located near the central plaza, it's the perfect place to grab a quick bite or sit down for a tasty meal.

From classic Margherita to pepperoni, veggie supreme, and BBQ chicken, Mo’s Pizza offers a wide range of options to satisfy every appetite. All pizzas are made to order with fresh ingredients, a signature tomato sauce, and perfectly crisp crusts.

Prefer something smaller? Try the mini personal pizzas, pizza slices, or garlic breadsticks. Gluten-free bases and vegetarian options are also available to ensure everyone can enjoy a slice of the action.

With plenty of seating and friendly service, Mo’s Pizza is a great stop for lunch, dinner, or an afternoon snack. Combo meals and soft drinks are also available, and kids will love the animal-themed pizza boxes!

Open daily from 10:30 AM to 6:00 PM. Dine-in or take your pizza to go and enjoy it at one of our picnic areas like The Lost Forest.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

You will find Mo's Pizza opposite the Coffee Shop.
